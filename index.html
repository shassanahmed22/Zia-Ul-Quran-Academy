About Us

Zia ul Quran Academy

We provide an e-learning experience to students who can enlighten themselves from the knowledge of Quran through a remote platform. Our academy is the platform for individuals who desire to study Islam and the Quran online through distance learning.

We have created a comprehensive curriculum for learning the Quran and the fundamentals of Islam. To students from UK, USA, Canada, Australia or any part of the world, through our cutting-edge online Islamic classes, you can learn about Islam and gain firsthand experience.

Our Classes

We provide an attentive learning experience by allocating a 30-minute time slot to each student.

30 minutes for each student:
>> 20 minutes for Qaida/Quran
>> 10 minutes for duas and other Ibadah (Islamic Studies)

Our Tutors

Our tutors are highly motivated professionals who understand how to maintain a positive relationship with students in a welcoming atmosphere to achieve the greatest outcomes.

- Quran & Urdu instructors with formal training in teaching online
- Fluent in English, Arabic, and Urdu
- Highly motivated and aware of professional challenges
- Driven to pursue the best possible outcomes
